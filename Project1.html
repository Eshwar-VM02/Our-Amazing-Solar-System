Solar system Overview

The solar system consists of the Sun, eight planets, five officially recognized dwarf planets, hundreds of moons, and thousands of asteroids and comets.

  • Planets: The eight planets include Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.
  • Dwarf Planets: Notable dwarf planets include Pluto, Eris, Haumea, Makemake, and Ceres.
  • Other Objects: The solar system also contains numerous smaller objects such as comets and asteroids, with over 796,000 asteroids and 3,500 comets identified.
